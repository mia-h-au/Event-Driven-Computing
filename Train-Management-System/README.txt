DESCRIPTIONS:

Train Management System - to manage an interlocking for a rail yard that has 13 sections of track, satisfying the following requirements:
- Allowing trains to pass through the rail yard without colliding,
- Preventing deadlock,
- Making efficient use of the yard.


INSTRUCTIONS TO RUN THE CODE:

Open click-me.html in browser. 
Click Add Train to add a new train to the rail yard. 
Click on a train to make it move forward.
